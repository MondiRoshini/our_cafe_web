1. Choco-chip Cookie

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Granulated sugar
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla extract
  • Egg
  • Baking soda

Recipe:

Follow the following steps-

  1. You’re going to start by melting the butter. The butter shouldn’t be sizzling hot. If you’re not sure how hot is too hot, you’ll want it about 90% melted, and then you’ll whisk it until it finishes melting.
  2. Once the butter has melted, whisk in the sugars until well-combined.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract and the egg.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in just until combined. You don’t want to overmix the dough.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips, and then scoop the dough. Place about 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheet.

2. Brownie Bites

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract

Recipe:

Follow the following steps-

  1. Melt the butter and chocolate in a saucepan. Set aside to cool.
  2. Mix the eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt until thick and well-combined.
  3. Mix in the chocolate until combined. Add the dry ingredients and stir in just until incorporated.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Divide the batter between the prepared muffin tins.
  6. Bake.
  7. Let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Remove the brownie bites to a wire cooling rack to cool.

Now we have some chocolaty brownie bites yum!yum!

3. Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • Ice cream(ideally full-fat, choose any flavour)
  • Milk
  • Add-ins(syrups, fruits, nuts,etc)
  • Toppings(sprinkles, cherries, etc)

Recipe:

Follow the following steps-

  1. Soften the ice cream and chill the glass. Take the ice cream out of the freezer for 10 to 15 minutes before use.
  2. At the same time, place your glass in the freezer. This will keep the shake cold when you serve it.
  3. Measure the ingredients. 1 cup ice cream to 1/4 cup milk is a great ratio.
  4. Blend the shake. In a blender, combine the milk and ice cream and blend until smooth.
  5. Add any your mix-ins and blend them in to your desired consistency.
  6. Serve. Pour the shake into the chilled glass and add desired toppings onto it like a scoop of ice cream, sprinkles, etc./li>

Have you thick, smooth, yummy milkshake!
